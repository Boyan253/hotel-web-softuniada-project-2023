About Us

Discover Meeting Point Hotels. World-class hospitality

F.O.U.R offer affordable holidays with a wide array of lodging options at individual hotels managed by a world-class hospitality company. We welcome couples, families and groups to enjoy a laid-back ambiance and resort-style accommodation in comfortable seaside holiday settings just moments from the beach in Turkey and the Canary Islands. Whether your ideal holiday setting is on the shores of the Mediterranean or a getaway to the warm Atlantic shores of Canary Island, F.O.U.R are finely crafted to serve as your gateway to unforgettable experiences.
